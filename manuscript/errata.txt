## Reporting Errata

If you think you've found an error relating to spelling, grammar, or anything else that's currently holding this book back from being the best it can be, please visit the book's [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Digital-Forensics-Discord-Server/TheHitchhikersGuidetoDFIRExperiencesFromBeginnersandExperts/issues) and create an Issue detailing the error you've found.
